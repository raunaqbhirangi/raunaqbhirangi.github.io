About Me
Hi there! I’m Raunaq and I am a PhD student in the Robotics Institute at Carnegie Mellon University advised by Prof. Abhinav Gupta and Prof. Carmel Majidi. I am interested in developing algorithms and hardware to enhance robots’ capability to operate in unstructured physical environments. To that end, I am currently working on ReSkin, a soft magnetic skin capable of large area tactile sensing!
